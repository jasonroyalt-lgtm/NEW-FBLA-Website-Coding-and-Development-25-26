FMHS History

At Fruita Monument High school, we have provided a top quality, one-of-a-kind education since our founding in 1905. We aim to cultivate innovation, achievement, and passion through a learner-centered environment. With a district-high 28 AP classes, over 30 career and technical education (CTE) courses, and a variety of dual enrollment through Colorado Mesa University, our course selection is unmatched. this aligns with our high expectations further emphasized by our 93% graduation rate. All of this to say: It's great to be a Fruita Monument Wildcat!

About LOCATE

Where nearly 1700 students and staff at Fruita Monument High School saw piles of lost clothes and belongings, we at LOCATE saw an opportunity. Understanding the awkwardness of scowering through others' lost items, we wanted to create a solution that was not only easy on school administrators, but that also minimized the need to search the whole school--or even through other people's stuff--to find what you lost. Through this, FMHS LOCATE was born. Our searchable listing and item claiming system save students valuable time and energy, and allow them to focus on the busy lives and involvement in everything that makes Fruita Monument what it is.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I claim an item?

It's simple--go to the "search item" page, and when you find an item that you have lost, click on the "claim item" button below it. You will fill out a short form, and that's all you need to do!


2. What information do I have to provide to claim an item?

Only 2 things: your first & last name, and your District 51 email.


3. Can I edit an item report that I filled out, after it has been approved?

Unfortunately, no. You are unable to change an item report once you have filled out the form, so be careful with what you put in the description!


4. How can I be sure that someone else doesn't claim my item?

Our school admin are extremly careful about this. Not only do we have the item claim form in place for this exact reason, but staff familiar with LOCATE will also make sure to ask questions about the item, to ensure that the item doesn't fall into the wrong hands.


5. I have filled out the form to report an item. What now?

Once your report has been approved, take the item to the lost and found, and let admin take care of the rest!


6. How do I claim in person once I have claimed it on the website?

Once your claim has been approved by admin, show up to the lost and found, give them your school ID as confirmation of your claim on the website, and you'll have your item back!


7. How long before items are donated or given away?

We offload items at the end of each semester, so make sure to pick up items before then!


8. How can I know if an item I lost was found?

Make sure to check here on the website every day, to see if your item is currently in the lost and found!


9. How will I know when my item report was approved?

If you check again on the website at some point later during the day, you should be able to see the item your reported if your report was approved.
